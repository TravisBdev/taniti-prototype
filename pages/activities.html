Adventure & Exploration

Taniti is an adventurer’s paradise, offering a wide range of activities to immerse you in the island’s natural beauty. Hiking enthusiasts will love exploring the trails that wind through lush rainforests, leading up to breathtaking views of Mount Tamaru, the island’s active volcano. For those who prefer the water, Taniti’s crystal-clear beaches are perfect for snorkeling, where vibrant coral reefs and exotic marine life await beneath the surface. Surfing is another popular activity along the western coast, where gentle waves welcome beginners and offer more challenging swells for seasoned surfers. Sailing around the island is a must, providing unforgettable views of the coastline, and guests can charter boats for fishing expeditions or sunset cruises.

Family Fun & Romantic Getaways

Taniti is a family-friendly destination with activities for visitors of all ages. Families can rent scooters to explore the island at their own pace, stopping by secluded beaches or local attractions. Fishing tours, both off the pier and deep-sea, offer a fun way to bond over the thrill of the catch, while younger guests can enjoy afternoons at the local arcade or miniature golf course. For dining, Taniti offers a variety of family-friendly restaurants featuring fresh, local seafood, Pan-Asian cuisine, and American-style meals that cater to all tastes. Couples looking for a romantic getaway will find intimate, candlelit dinners at waterfront restaurants, where the sound of the waves and stunning sunset views set the perfect mood.

Nightlife & Entertainment

As the sun sets, Taniti transforms into a lively hub for adult travelers. The island is home to several bars and pubs, including a popular microbrewery located in the heart of Taniti City. Visitors can unwind with a craft beer or tropical cocktail while enjoying live music or mingling with fellow travelers. For those looking to dance the night away, the Grand Palm Hotel is home to the island’s only nightclub, offering DJ performances, themed nights, and a vibrant atmosphere perfect for a memorable night out. Whether you're looking for a quiet drink at a beach bar or a lively dance floor, Taniti’s nightlife has something for everyone.
